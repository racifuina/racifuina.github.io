Gan Hillel Pre-School

The main purpose of the app is to allow the parents to have access to the activities of the year, they can chat with other parents with kids in the same classroom without the need of sharing phone numbers or emails.

It allows the teachers to share a monthly calendar with the activities of the month for their classroom.

It has many other features like: Push notifications, classrom calendar, school calendar, financial alerts, hability to have different profiles if the parents have more than one kid in the school.

This app is avaliable on the AppStore and Google Play

  • Client: Gan Hillel Pre-School
  • Date: November 2015
  • Service: iOS/Android App - Backend server

TeleEntry

This app is the companion for TeleEntry sold by GTBilt

It allows neighbors from a neighborhood to create access codes for their visitors.

The app has access to the phone book to gather visitors information easier and enable sharing the access code automatically by email if it's avaliable.

It allows them to know exactly when the visitor went through the main gate with a push notification.

This app is avaliable on the AppStore and Google Play

  • Client: GTBilt
  • Date: March 2016
  • Service: iOS/Android App Development

TeleTemp

This app allows users to manage their TeleTemp devices sold by GTBilt. It has a dashboard with the list of the connected devices, showing the status of the temperature and humidity sensors. The status is updated in real time.

It uses push notifications to alert the users when an alarm has been triggered.

This app is avaliable on the AppStore and Google Play

  • Client: GTBilt
  • Date: March 2016
  • Service: iOS/Android App Development

TeleWeight

This is a platform created for a plastic factory.

The main purpose of it is for the managers to be able to check the status of the factory's daily production in real time.

The server comunicates with the connected devices and the operator's tablets to gather the information, and it displays them in the web platform.

The website shows charts about the products, and allows them to export the information to an Excel file.

The android app is configured to be a kiosk app, blocking all other apps or configuration.

This is a private platform, and it is not avaliable for download on Google Play.

  • Client: GTBilt
  • Date: May 2016
  • Service: iOS/Android App Development
